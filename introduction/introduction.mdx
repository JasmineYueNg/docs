## How do I add an integration to my project?

### Step 1: Access the MCP Tool Panel

<div className="flex items-start gap-3 p-4 bg-blue-50 rounded-lg mb-4 border border-blue-200">
  <div className="p-1 bg-blue-500 rounded">
    <Settings className="w-4 h-4 text-white" />
  </div>
  <div>
    <p className="text-gray-700">
      The <strong>MCP Tool</strong> entry point is located at the <strong>bottom left</strong> of the input box on the page creation screen. Click it to expand the list of available tools and integrations for your current project.
    </p>
  </div>
</div>

### Step 2: Add Your Integration

You can add integrations by **combining natural language prompts with MCP Tool actions**:

    </div>

    </div>
  </div>
</div>
