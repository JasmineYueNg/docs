## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I change plans anytime?" icon="arrows-up-down">
    **Yes!** Upgrade or downgrade anytime. When you upgrade mid-cycle, you get the Credit difference immediately. When you downgrade, changes take effect at your next billing cycle.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What happens if I run out of Credits?" icon="battery-empty">
    You can't start new AI tasks until you get more Credits. But you can still view, edit manually, publish, and download existing projects. Upgrade your plan or wait for your monthly reset.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Do unused Credits carry over?" icon="calendar">
    **Yes.** Credits never expire. If you don't use all your monthly Credits, they stay in your account forever.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I cancel anytime?" icon="xmark">
    **Absolutely.** Cancel anytime with no penalties. You keep access to paid features until the end of your current billing period.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Is there a free trial for Pro?" icon="gift">
    New users start with **500 free Credits** to explore YouWare. This lets you test all features before committing to a paid plan.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What's the difference between Pro and Ultra?" icon="scale-balanced">
    **Credits & Rewinds.** Ultra gives you 3x more Credits (10,000 vs 3,000) and 3x more Credit Rewinds (100 vs 30) per month, plus early access to new features. Perfect for heavy users.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Do you offer team or business plans?" icon="users">
    Not yet, but team features are coming soon. Enterprise customers with custom needs can contact our sales team.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
