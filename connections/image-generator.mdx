## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What image generation model is used?">
    The Image Generator tool uses Flux, a state-of-the-art text-to-image generation model known for its high-quality outputs and accurate prompt interpretation.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How much does it cost to generate images?">
    Image generation consumes credits due to the computational power required. The exact cost depends on your subscription plan. Check your plan's credit pricing in the billing section for detailed information.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What makes a good prompt?">
    Be specific and descriptive for best results:
    
    - Include details about **style** (e.g., "minimalist," "photorealistic," "watercolor")
    - Specify **composition** (e.g., "centered," "close-up," "wide angle")
    - Mention **colors** and **mood** (e.g., "warm sunset tones," "dramatic lighting")
    - Add **context** about the subject (e.g., "a modern office space with plants")
    
    **Example good prompt:** "A minimalist blue whale logo with clean lines on white background, modern tech startup style"
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How long does it take to generate an image?">
    Image generation typically takes a few seconds to complete. The exact time may vary depending on:
    - Prompt complexity and detail level
    - Current system load and queue position
    - Image resolution and quality settings
    
    Most images are ready within 5-15 seconds.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What image formats and sizes are supported?">
    Generated images are provided in standard web-optimized formats (PNG/JPEG). The resolution is suitable for most web applications, blog posts, and digital marketing materials.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Tip>
For best results, iterate on your prompts. Start with a basic description, generate an image, then refine your prompt based on the output to get closer to your vision.
</Tip>
