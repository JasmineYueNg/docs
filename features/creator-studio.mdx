View your project performance, earnings, and revenue data in one place. Track views, credits spent by others on your projects, and manage withdrawals.

## Access Creator Studio

Click your avatar in the upper right corner → select **Creator Studio**

Creator Studio has two sections:
- **Dashboard** — project performance and metrics
- **Monetization** — earnings and withdrawal management

## Dashboard

<img src="/images/creator-studio-dashboard.png" alt="Creator Studio Dashboard showing project analytics and performance metrics" />

View all your projects' performance data. Track how many people use your projects and how much credit they consume.

### Key Metrics

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Total Views" icon="eye">
    Total views across all your projects
  </Card>
  <Card title="Credits Spent" icon="zap">
    Total Credits consumed by others using your projects
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Project Details

Each project shows:
- **Project name & publication date**
- **Credits Spent** — total Credits consumed
- **Times Used** — how many times invoked
- **Views** — number of visits
- **Built On / Saves** — times remixed or saved

<img src="/images/creator-studio-projects.png" alt="Creator Studio project details table showing individual project performance metrics" />

## Monetization

Manage your earnings and withdrawals. View balance, transaction history, and request payouts.

<img src="/images/creator-studio-monetization.png" alt="Creator Studio monetization panel showing earnings and transaction history" />

### Account Overview

- **Account Balance**: Your current balance
- **Total Earnings**: Cumulative earnings
- **Withdrawn / Processing**: Withdrawal history
- **Request Withdrawal**: Initiate a payout (minimum $50 USD)

### Transaction History

View all transactions with details: source, amount, payment method, status, and date. Updated daily.

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How long does a withdrawal take?">
    Withdrawal timing depends on your payment method. Check your transaction history for status updates.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What counts as 'Credits Spent'?">
    When someone else uses your project (generates with your project, remixes it, or runs any AI feature in it), that's when Credits are consumed.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I withdraw to any country?">
    Withdrawal methods depend on your region and payment provider integration. Check your account settings for available options.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What's the maximum I can withdraw?">
    No maximum. You can withdraw your entire balance as long as it's at least $50 USD.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
