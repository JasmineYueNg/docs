## Installation and Usage

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find the Extension">
    Search for "YouWare" in your editor's extension marketplace
  </Step>
  <Step title="Install & Login">
    Install the extension and log in to your YouWare account
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish Your Project">
    Open your project and click Publish Project
  </Step>
  <Step title="You're Live!">
    Get the link, your project is now live
  </Step>
</Steps>
