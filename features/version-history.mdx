## How to Rewind

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open History">
    In the editor toolbar, click the clock icon. The history drawer slides in with your saved versions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Preview a Version">
    Pick any entry. The preview updates instantly so you can compare layouts, copy, and assets.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Restore">
    Hit **Restore this version**. We apply it right away and snapshot the current state, just in case you change your mind again.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Use the version notes column to leave yourself quick reminders—"Added Halloween banner" or "Clean layout"—so you can find the right point in seconds.
</Tip>

## When to Use It

- Tried a bold edit and want to jump back fast
